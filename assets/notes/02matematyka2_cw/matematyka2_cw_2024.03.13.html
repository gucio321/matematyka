Metoda czynnika całkującego¶

\[\begin{split} y' + p(x) = q(x) \\ y' \mu(x) + \mu(x)p(x)y = q(x) \mu(x) \\ (y*\mu(x))' = q(x) \mu(x) \\ \mu(x) = e^{\int p(x) dx} \end{split}\]
